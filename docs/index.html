Swiss Participation in FP7 and Horizon 2020

Background

Horizon 2020 (H2020) is the part of EU’s Multiannual Financial Framework for 2014–2020 (MFF 2014–2020) that is allocated to research and innovation. H2020 is also known as the 8th Framework Programme in Research and Innovation (FP8). H2020 represents nearly 10% of the MFF 2014–2020 budget.

Switzerland has been fully associated to the EU’s Research Programmes since 2004 (see Swiss Participation in European Research Framework Programmes).



For official statistics, please visit Facts and Figures on the Swiss Participation in the EU-Framework Programmes for Research and Innovation.
